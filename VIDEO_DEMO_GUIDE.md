## üé§ Audio Tips

### Get Clear Audio

1. **Microphone Position**
   - 6-8 inches from mouth
   - Slightly off to the side
   - Use pop filter if available

2. **Environment**
   - Quiet room
   - Close windows
   - Turn off fans/AC during recording
   - Use soft furnishings to reduce echo

3. **Recording Settings**
   - Sample rate: 48kHz
   - Bit depth: 24-bit
   - Format: WAV or FLAC (lossless)

4. **Post-Processing**
   - Normalize audio levels
   - Remove background noise
   - Add slight compression
   - Use Audacity (free) for editing
